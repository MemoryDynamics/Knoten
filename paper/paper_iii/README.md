# Paper III - Quantum and Standard-Model Programme (planned)

This directory is a placeholder for a later companion programme. It is not a
result of Paper 0 and should not be cited as evidence for quantum mechanics,
particle spectra, gauge structure, physical constants, or Standard-Model
phenomenology.

Paper III is reserved for questions that can only be approached after the
mathematical anchor in Paper 0 and the propagation/spacetime programme in
Paper II have been made sharper:

- whether any controlled continuum or small-noise limit yields a
  Schrodinger-like effective equation;
- whether relaxation or stability scales can be calibrated against a physical
  mass scale rather than used only as mass-like proxies;
- whether constants such as hbar or c can be introduced by empirical
  calibration rather than postulated;
- whether multi-knot interactions support structures analogous to spin,
  statistics, gauge coupling, or particle classes.

The current repository does not claim that these structures emerge.
